FBMeet

Create small events quickly in Facebook Chat!

Get FBMeet at the Chrome Store

What

FBMeet makes it easier and faster to schedule small events with your friends! Ever wanted to create an event while chatting with frieds but you don't want to fill a form full of details of an event in which you don't even know where is gonna be yet? That's what FBMeet was created for.

In Facebook, while chatting with a friend you want to meet, just look up the small chat window, press the calendar icon, and you have an event ready to be created, you can provide a name, or leave the default "Meetup" and choose when it's gonna happen or leave "Soon". After pressing Ok, an event page has already been created, then you can invite more friends dragging them from the chat friend's list, immediatly after dropping they'll be invited to the event!
